GE Refrigerator Repairs We Offer

1. Compressor Repair and Replacement
The compressor is the “heart” of your refrigerator, and a failure can stop the entire cooling system. We specialize in compressor repair and replacement, using original parts like WR87X10252 GE compressor. Our technicians use advanced diagnostic tools like Fieldpiece MR45 recovery machine and JB DV-6E vacuum pumps to ensure precision during refrigerant recovery and recharging. Fixing the compressor will get your cooling back to normal and prevent costly replacements.

2. Ice Maker Repair
A faulty ice maker can be caused by clogged water lines, failed motors, or faulty thermostats. We use parts like WR30X10093 ice maker assembly and WR57X10051 water inlet valve for repair. For diagnosis, we use Fluke 87V multimeters to test circuits and water flow gauges to find clogs and get you back to making perfect ice fast.

3. Temperature Control System Repair
Temperature unevenness means problems with the temperature control system, such as faulty thermostats, sensors, or control boards. We replace parts like WR55X10025 temperature sensor and WR50X10068 defrost thermostat. Our technicians use Smart Sensor Probes and UEi DL429 HVAC clamp meters to find temperature fluctuations and repair control components quickly.

4. Door Seal Replacement
A worn-out door seal lets cold air escape, straining the compressor and increasing your energy bills. We replace faulty seals using parts like WR24X10228 door gasket and Malco C5R crimping tools to get a tight seal. This repair will save you energy and extend your refrigerator’s life.

5. Defrost System Repair
Excessive frost buildup is usually caused by defrost system failures, including defective heaters, timers, or sensors. We use parts like WR51X10053 defrost heater and WR9X502 defrost timer. For diagnosis, our technicians use infrared thermometers and HVAC megohm testers to get airflow and cooling back to normal.

6. Water Dispenser and Filter Repair
Problems with the water dispenser or filters are usually caused by clogs, damaged lines, or faulty valves. We replace parts like MWF water filter cartridge and WR57X10032 water valve to ensure clean filtered water. We use PEX crimp tools and PEX cutters to handle water lines with precision and prevent leaks.

7. Lighting System Repair
A faulty lighting system can be caused by burnt-out bulbs, faulty switches, or damaged wiring. We use parts like WR55X11132 LED light assembly and WR23X10530 door switch for repair. We use Klein Tools wire stripper and Milwaukee battery-powered testers to get your refrigerator lit and functional.

8. Smart Features Troubleshooting

9. Refrigerant Leak Repair
A refrigerant leak means your refrigerator is not cooling efficiently. We find and fix leaks with Inficon D-TEK Select refrigerant leak detectors and refill the system with R-134a refrigerant. We replace parts like WR87X29275 refrigerant tubing for a long-term solution.

10. Evaporator and Condenser Fan Repair
Faulty evaporator and condenser fans cause airflow issues, uneven cooling, and overheating. We replace fans with parts like WR60X10220 evaporator fan motor and WR60X29099 condenser fan assembly. We use Wahl cordless screwdrivers and TrueFlow airflow testers to get it installed and running right.

13. Control Board Repair

14. Drainage System Cleaning and Repair
